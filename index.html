Biography

Meet DERIN ATAY, a newcomer in the music industry who is 25 years old and based in Berlin, Germany. Though originally from Izmir, Turkey, she has called Germany home since she was 14 years old. As a former artist manager and now as a musician, DERIN ATAY draws inspiration from a variety of influences, resulting in her unique sound. She looks to the soulful melodies of Amy Winehouse, the catchy pop sounds of Dua Lipa, the boldness of Shirin David, and the insightful lyricism of J. Cole. Growing up, hip-hop, rap, and R&B were her favorite genres, largely due to her brother's musical preferences. At 25, DERIN ATAY is ready to leave her mark on the music world and connect with fans from all walks of life. Her upcoming releases promise a captivating blend of genres, heartfelt lyrics, and an authentic expression of her personal journey. Keep an ear out for DERIN ATAY's upcoming releases as she embarks on an exciting musical journey that will surely resonate with her listeners.
